Akata Witch

Climax

The climax of Akata Witch occurs when Sunny and her friends must face the evil Leopard Society. The Leopard Society is a group of Leopard People who have turned to evil and are trying to destroy the world. Sunny and her friends must use all of their powers to defeat the Leopard Society and save the world.
