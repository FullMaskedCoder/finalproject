Akata Witch

Rising Action

The rising action of Akata Witch occurs when Sunny Nwazue and her friends must learn to control their magical abilities and face the challenges of being Leopard People. Sunny and her friends must also learn to work together to defeat the evil Leopard Society, who are trying to destroy the world. The rising action of the novel is full of action, adventure, and suspense.
