Akata Witch

About Akata Witch

Akata Witch is a fantasy novel by Nnedi Okorafor. It was first published in 2011 and tells the story of Sunny Nwazue, a 12-year-old Nigerian girl who discovers that she is a Leopard Person, a powerful being with magical abilities. The novel has been praised for its strong characters, its exploration of African culture and mythology, and its positive message about self-acceptance.

About the Author

The author of Akata Witch is Nnedi Okorafor. She was born in the United States to Nigerian parents, and she grew up in both countries. She is a graduate of the University of Michigan and the Clarion Workshop for Science Fiction and Fantasy Writers.
