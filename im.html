Akata Witch

Inciting Moment

The inciting moment of Akata Witch occurs when Sunny Nwazue discovers that she is a Leopard Person. Sunny is a 12-year-old girl who lives in Aba, Nigeria. She is a bright and curious girl, but she is also shy and insecure. Sunny's life changes when she discovers that she has magical abilities. She is able to see and talk to spirits, and she can also use her powers to heal people. Sunny's discovery that she is a Leopard Person sets her on a journey of self-discovery and adventure.
